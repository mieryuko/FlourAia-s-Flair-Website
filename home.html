Frequently Asked Questions
Do you have a physical store? ▼
No, FlourAia's Flair is a small, home-based business. But we are hoping to expand on the idea at the soonest.
Do you accept Cash on Delivery (COD)? ▼
Yes we do, especially for self pick-up orders. However, we ask that you follow our downpayment policy.
Do you accept custom designs/orders? ▼
Yes, we do! You can customize the cookie boxes, cookie pouches, cake designs, and flavors (Build a Box). However, certain limitations are applied. Please contact us for further details!
Do you accept rush orders? ▼
Yes, we accept 1-2 rushed orders at a time, but customers are adviced to place their orders in advance. This is because the baker's availability is limited.
Are the prices indicated in your menu the same as the prices for all your cakes? ▼
No, the prices listed are the base prices. Additional charges may apply in customized orders.
