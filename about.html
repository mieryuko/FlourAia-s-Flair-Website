Business Description
Founded in 2023, FlourAia's Flair is a home-based small business located in Pateros, Metro Manila, specializing in gourmet cookies and desserts. We cater to students, working professionals, and all types of events, big or small. Our mission is to bring comfort and joy to people through our delicious treats, satisfying cravings, and helping them celebrate life’s special moments. At FlourAia's Flair, every bite is crafted with love and passion, making your celebrations even more memorable and sweet.

I was always clear with myself—I wanted to have a coffee shop where I could sell pastries and fresh flowers every day, even though I had no idea where to find those pastries and cakes.
Fast forward to today, FlourAia’s Flair started as a simple “past-time” during the pandemic, when everyone was stuck at home. It eventually grew into an expensive hobby that required lots of equipment and costly ingredients. I used to share my cakes and pastries with loved ones, and their positive feedback motivated me to keep going. As I nourished them with baked goods, their kind words fueled my confidence to turn my passion into a business. At first, I was scared and hesitant—it took years of courage to admit that I wanted more people to try my creations.
Then, one school event challenged me to step out of my comfort zone, and that’s when FlourAia’s Flair was born. We started by selling simple brownies topped with almonds and walnuts. Thanks to the love and support from customers, and the savings I earned, I was able to expand our menu to include cookies and cakes. FlourAia’s Flair is just a one-year-old business aiming to serve more customers and bake more delicious treats. We love the idea of seeing our cakes on the tables of celebrants, knowing they trust us with their special days. While we are still a small business, our vision to celebrate and spread sweetness remains big.
